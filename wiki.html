Description

Welcome to Bread Clicker!

Create your bread empire by making dough! How do you do this, you may ask? Well, just click on the bread in the middle to start making dough. Click. Click some more. Tired, aren't you? Have some help! Either upgrade your bread, buy spreads, or get an upgrade. Let's explore your options.

Buy Breads

Up your game with a two-in-one package along with a shiny new look to your bread! It increases your DPS, or Dough Per Second. It will also increase your DPC, or Dough Per Click. Very simple stuff.

Buy Spreads

Increase your DPS by filling your collection with sweet and savory spreads! These make every other DPS purchase a little more valuable.

Buy Upgrades

Increase your DPS by having bread being made for you! You can purchase appliances, people, businesses, and plots of land to increase your DPS earnings. Upgrades can be bought multiple times as well! The only limit is how much money you have.
